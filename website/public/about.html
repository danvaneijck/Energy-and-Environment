General

Energy and Environment is the premier source for news and insights into the energy and environment sector in New Zealand.
The bulletin covers the complex sectors with a focus on politics, policy and Parliament with coverage of legislation, business and legal news as well as other issues.

Publishing

The Energy and Environment bulletin is published weekly with at least 45 editions a year. There are also occasional interim editions to cover the emergence of breaking, important news.
Until December 2018, Energy and Environment was published on Wednesday. From January 2019 the publishing date moved to Thursday afternoon. Subscribers who have not received their copy of the bulletin by 5pm should get in contact with the editor via energyandenvironmentnz@gmail.com

The bulletin is provided in PDF format, but subscribers who prefer other formats can be accommodated.
Articles are also available via the website -https://energyandenvironment.net.nz/home.html. This a subscriber only service and contains archives of material from previous editions of Energy and Environment. Past articles can be found by date in the archive, and by subject through the search engine.

History

Energy and Environment was published for more than 15 years as part of the Trans-Tasman stable of publications founded by Ian Templeton. This was taken over by Max Bowden and unfortunately the company was placed in liquidation in 2017 and wound up.

In 2018, Energy and Environment was purchased from those who had taken over the distressed assets and now operates as a separate publication. Trans-Tasman is still published by Tony Doe who can be contacted at tdoe@transtasman.co.nz.

Energy and Environment is now written by a group of people, some of whom used to work for the former Trans-Tasman publication. It also has additional contributors all with experience and in-depth knowledge of the sectors they cover.

Energy and Environment is published by Calcamrya Ltd and edited and curated by Ian Llewellyn.
